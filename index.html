Welcome to a website about Running. For thousands of years running has been the world’s most accessible sport. As the most fundamental configuration of the human motor skill, running is a cheap and fast-growing sport. Aspects of running range from different types of speed from sprinting to jogging. Running helps promote health benefits and improves overall the physical/mental condition of an individual. Interested in becoming a runner? All you need to start is a shirt, shorts, and running shoes!

Training

Without any form of training, running for a specific amount of time or speed is a difficult task to perform. Proper training is extremely vital for an athlete’s success on and off the track. While undertraining correlates athletes to never reach their peak, overtraining can cause athletes to peak early on which later result to major burnout. An athlete overtraining can also lead to minor or severe injuries due to joint/body overuse which could set an individual out for days/weeks/months away from the sport.

Shoes/Spikes

In the world of running, the proper shoe to train in often called “trainers”. There's a number of different types of training shoes that specialize for running. Brands such as Nike, Asics, Saucony, and New Balance are popular footwear within the running world. On the other hand, spikes are specifically for racing. The term “spike” comes from the protrusion inserted at the bottom of the shoe to increase traction. The model of spikes varies appearance depending on the specific distance it’s made for. As distance spikes have more of a balance between the midfoot and heel, sprinting spikes are mostly elevated entirely on the midfoot. Spikes are made lighter than trainers.

Competition

In track, there are a numerous number of different events that athletes can participate in. However, in track there are two seasons that take place at various times of the year (Indoor/Outdoor). Indoor occurs during the winter, individual events that can be ran indoors are 60m, 60m hurdles, 200m, 300m, 400m, 500m, 600m, 800m, 1000m, Mile, 3000m, and 5000m. Outdoors 100m, 100m hurdles, 200m, 400m, 400m hurdles, 800m, 1500m, 3000m, 3000m steeplechase, and 5000m can be ran. Other than track, Cross country running takes place from the summer to the end of November.
